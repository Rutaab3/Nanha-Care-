About Me

Hello! I'm Amina, an after-school program specialist with 4 years of experience helping school-age children with their academic needs. I'm fluent in both Urdu and English, which allows me to effectively support children in both languages.

My approach focuses on making homework time productive and stress-free while ensuring children understand their schoolwork thoroughly. I specialize in creating personalized learning plans that cater to each child's unique learning style and academic needs.

I believe in fostering a positive learning environment that builds children's confidence in their academic abilities while helping them develop strong study habits and time management skills.
