About Me

Hello! I'm Zara, a compassionate and attentive babysitter specializing in infant and toddler care. With 2 years of professional experience, I provide gentle, nurturing care that helps babies feel safe and loved.

My approach is based on gentle parenting principles, focusing on creating a warm and responsive environment for your little ones. I'm fluent in Urdu and comfortable communicating with parents in their preferred language.

I take pride in being patient, observant, and attuned to each child's unique needs and cues. Whether it's feeding, diaper changes, or helping establish healthy sleep routines, I provide reliable and loving care for your precious little ones.
