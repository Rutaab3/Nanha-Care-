Our Verification Process

Ensuring only the most trustworthy caregivers join our platform

CNIC Verification

We validate each babysitter's Computerized National Identity Card through NADRA's verification system to confirm their identity and citizenship status.

Police Clearance Certificate

All babysitters must provide a valid Police Clearance Certificate from their local police station, ensuring no criminal record exists.

In-Person Interview

Our team conducts comprehensive face-to-face interviews assessing childcare knowledge, experience, and interpersonal skills.

Training & Certification

All approved babysitters complete our specialized childcare training program covering safety protocols, first aid, and child development.
