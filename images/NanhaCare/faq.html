Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from Pakistani parents

How do you verify your babysitters?
Each babysitter undergoes CNIC verification, a police clearance, and an in-person interview before being listed on NanhaCare.
Can I request the same babysitter again?
Yes, if your preferred babysitter is available, you can book them again through your account dashboard.
What if I have an emergency?
Use the emergency bell icon on every page to instantly access emergency numbers and initiate a call.
Do you offer services in all cities?
We currently serve major cities including Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad, with plans to expand further.
What products do you offer?
We offer a curated selection of baby essentials including food, clothing, hygiene items, skincare, and toys.
