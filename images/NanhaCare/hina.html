About Me

Hello! I'm Hina, a Montessori-trained early childhood educator with 6 years of experience working with toddlers and preschoolers. My approach combines Montessori principles with creative learning techniques to foster independence and curiosity in young children.

Fluent in both Urdu and English, I provide bilingual education that helps children develop language skills naturally through play and daily activities. I believe in creating a nurturing environment where children can explore, discover, and learn at their own pace.

My goal is to help children develop foundational skills while maintaining their natural love for learning. I specialize in creating hands-on, engaging activities that promote cognitive, social, and emotional development.
