About NanhaCare

Empowering Parents. Enriching Childhood.

Our Purpose

NanhaCare was created to provide safe, trustworthy, and accessible childcare solutions to parents across Pakistan.

Whether you're a working parent or a stay-at-home guardian, our platform is designed to support your journey with verified babysitters and helpful resources.

Our Journey

From small beginnings to a national network of trusted caregivers.

Born out of a need for secure and local care options, NanhaCare began as a small initiative in Karachi. With overwhelming support from families and caregivers, we've grown into a national network that connects trusted babysitters with loving homes.

Our Core Values

What makes NanhaCare different

Safety First

Every babysitter undergoes rigorous background checks, interviews, and training to ensure your child's safety.

Compassionate Care

We select caregivers who genuinely love children and are passionate about their development.

Innovative Solutions

Using technology to create seamless experiences for parents and fair opportunities for caregivers.

Our Mission

To simplify parenting by offering reliable childcare services, insightful products, and expert advice, all in one place.

We believe that every parent deserves peace of mind when it comes to their child's care. Our mission is to create a community where families can find trustworthy support and caregivers can find meaningful work.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every parent feels supported, every child receives loving care, and technology bridges the gap between families and trusted helpers.

NanhaCare is more than just a service - we're building a movement to transform childcare in Pakistan. By combining rigorous vetting processes with compassionate matching, we're creating a new standard for what parents should expect from childcare services.
